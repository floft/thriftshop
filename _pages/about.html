--- title: "About Us" date: 2014-08-25 13:04:09 PDT permalink: /about/ --- The Beginning:
The Women's Ministries Division of the North Cascade Seventh-day Adventist Church (NCSDA) desired to help meet the needs of the people living in the Skagit Valley by raising funds for community service projects. They decided the best way to accomplish this goal was with the two-fold ministry of a thrift shop. A thrift shop would provide access to quality used clothes and other household items at affordable prices and at the same time generate funds that could then be given to community organizations specializing in meeting the needs of the local residents as well as projects reaching beyond the Skagit Valley.

The Women's Ministries Team shared their vision of opening a thrift shop with the NCSDA church board in September 1996. The church board embraced the vision and in October 1996, the Spruced Up Thrift Shop opened its doors on Spruce Street in downtown Burlington, Washington.
Expansion:
The store thrived under the management of these women and the overwhelming support of the church and community. Soon the space on Spruce Street was filled to overflowing and the possibility of relocating was considered. When a larger commercial space on Fairhaven Avenue became available, it was decided to move the thrift shop just around the corner from Spruce Street to Fairhaven Avenue, thus allowing the thrift shop to expand and bring in more donations and profits.

Other changes also took place and the thrift shop was no longer run solely by the Women's Ministries Division, but became its own entity, welcoming the added help from additional women, men, and youth.

Thanks to the support and generosity of the church and community, the Spruced Up Thrift Shop continues to serve the residents of the Skagit Valley and beyond. In order to meet a growing need for a local supplier of vegetarian products, the Spruced Up Thrift Shop now carries a variety of canned and frozen vegetarian products.
The Future:
We are thankful for the opportunity to continue fulfilling our mission of reaching out to all the poeple of the Skagit Valley and surrounding areas and are always looking for new ways to meet the needs of our community. We hope you will stop by soon and see our ever growing and always changing inventory.
