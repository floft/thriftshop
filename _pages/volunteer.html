We thank the many volunteers who donate hundreds of hours each month to keep the shop open and running smoothly, but there is always a need for more help. Help is especially needed in these areas:
- Merchandise preparation
- Inventory organization
- Cashier and Customer Service





For more information on how to volunteer your time and talents, please call the shop or stop by the store during regular store hours.
