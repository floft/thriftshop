Welcome to the Spruced Up Thrift Shop. We are privileged to be serving the people of the Skagit Valley and surrounding areas. It is our desire to help meet the needs of the communities in our valley by selling donated clothing and other household items at affordable prices and at the same time generating funds to support community service projects.








Please stop by our store and see our wide selection of clothing, toys, dishes, books, furniture, and much, much more.








Mission Statement:

The Spruced Up Thrift Shop was established as a non-profit outreach to provide goods and services of low cost to people in need. It is our purpose to follow Christ's example in reaching out to all people in our community.
